devtrackers.gg

Status: Active
As a player of many live-service games I often found myself wanting to keep up to day with what the game developers were posting about future plans, game bugs etc. Every game has its own way of communicating to its players. I built devtrackers.gg to aggregate all these sources into one feed for every game.
